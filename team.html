Team

Euclid high-z quasar team

We are an international collaboration within the quasar working package of the Euclid Consortium Primeval Universe Science Working Group, bringing together survey selection, image validation, spectroscopy, and catalog-building for the highest-redshift quasars found by Euclid.

This page is intended as a public-facing overview of the people behind the program, with room for member portraits, institutional links, and future project roles as the team roster expands.
